How do I take care of my Kenya Wood Carvings?

When it comes to taking care of your Kenya wood carvings, there are a few simple steps you can take to ensure that they last for years to come. Here are a few tips:

  1. Keep them away from direct sunlight and heat sources. This will help prevent the wood from drying out and cracking.
  2. Dust them regularly with a soft cloth or brush. This will help remove any dirt or dust that has accumulated on the carving.
  3. If the carving gets wet, dry it off immediately with a soft cloth. Water can cause the wood to swell and crack.
  4. If you notice any cracks or damage, take the carving to a professional for repair as soon as possible. Ignoring minor damage can cause major problems down the road.
